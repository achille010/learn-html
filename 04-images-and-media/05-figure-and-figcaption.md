# Figure and Figcaption

The `<figure>` and `<figcaption>` elements provide a semantic way to group content (like an image) with its caption.

## Basic Usage

```html
<figure>
    <img src="castle.jpg" alt="A large medieval castle">
    <figcaption>The Neuschwanstein Castle in Germany.</figcaption>
</figure>
```

## Why use `<figure>`?
While you could use a `<div>` and a `<p>`, the `<figure>` tag is more semantic. It tells the browser and search engines that the caption and image are directly related as a single unit.
