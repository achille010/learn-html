# Header and Footer

These tags represent the introduction and the conclusion of a page or section.

## `<header>`
The `<header>` usually contains logos, the site title, or a navigation menu.
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Do not confuse `<header>` with `<head>`. The `<head>` is for metadata, while `<header>` is for visible content.

## `<footer>`
The `<footer>` contains information like copyright, contact info, and legal links.

```html
<header>
    <h1>Welcome to My Blog</h1>
    <nav>...</nav>
</header>

<!-- Main content -->

<footer>
    <p>&copy; 2024 My Name</p>
</footer>
```
