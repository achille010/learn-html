# SEO Benefits of Semantic HTML

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of making your site easier for search engines to find. Semantic HTML is one of the easiest ways to improve your SEO.

## How it helps
1.  **Context**: Tags like `<article>` and `<h1>` tell Google exactly what the page is about.
2.  **Rich Snippets**: Proper use of tags like `<time>` and `<address>` can lead to "rich results" in search (like dates appearing directly in Google search results).
3.  **Indexing**: Search engines can index your site more accurately if they understand the hierarchy of your content.

## The Bottom Line
Writing clean, semantic HTML isn't just about accessibility; it's about making your site successful and visible to the world!
