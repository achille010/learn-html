# Article and Aside

These tags deal with piece-by-piece content and supplementary info.

## `<article>`
Use the `<article>` tag for self-contained content that could make sense on its own (like a blog post, a user review, or a news story).

## `<aside>`
The `<aside>` tag is for content that is related to the surrounding content but is separate (like a sidebar, a list of related links, or a pull-quote).

```html
<main>
    <article>
        <h2>The Secret of Great HTML</h2>
        <p>Semantic tags are the key...</p>
    </article>
    
    <aside>
        <h3>About the Author</h3>
        <p>Jane Doe is an expert dev...</p>
    </aside>
</main>
```
