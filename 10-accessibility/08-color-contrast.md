# Color Contrast

If your text doesn't stand out from the background, people with low vision or color blindness won't be able to read it.

## The Standards (WCAG 2.1)
-   **Level AA (Minimum)**: A contrast ratio of at least **4.5:1** for normal text and **3:1** for large text.
-   **Level AAA (Enhanced)**: A contrast ratio of at least **7:1** for normal text and **4.5:1** for large text.

## Contrast Checkers
Don't guess! Use tools to check your colors:
-   **Chrome DevTools**: The "Inspect" tool shows the contrast ratio for any text.
-   **WebAIM Contrast Checker**: A popular online tool for testing color pairs.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Never use color alone to convey meaning (e.g., "Click the green button to proceed"). Always use text or icons as well.
