# Why Accessibility Matters

Web accessibility is not just a "nice to have"—it is a core responsibility of every developer.

## The Pillars of a11y
1.  **Moral**: Everyone deserves equal access to information and services on the web.
2.  **Legal**: In many countries, accessibility is required by law (like the ADA or European Accessibility Act).
3.  **Business**: Accessible sites are often faster, have better SEO, and reach a wider audience (billions of people globally have some form of disability).

## Types of Disabilities to Consider
-   **Visual**: Blindness, low vision, color blindness.
-   **Motor**: Difficulty using a mouse or keyboard.
-   **Auditory**: Deafness or hearing loss.
-   **Cognitive**: Learning disabilities, memory issues, or distractions.
