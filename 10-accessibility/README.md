# Module 10: Accessibility (a11y)

Accessibility (often shortened to **a11y**) means making your websites usable by everyone, including people with disabilities.

## What's in this module?

1.  **Why Accessibility Matters**: Legal and moral foundations.
2.  **Semantic HTML and a11y**: The easiest way to be accessible.
3.  **ARIA Roles**: Adding meaning when HTML tags are not enough.
4.  **ARIA Labels and Descriptions**: Helping screen readers.
5.  **Keyboard Navigation**: Navigating without a mouse.
6.  **Focus Management**: Controlling the tab order.
7.  **Screen Reader Testing**: Seeing your site through a non-visual lens.
8.  **Color Contrast**: Ensuring your text is readable.
9.  **Accessible Forms**: A deep dive into inclusive inputs.
10. **WCAG Overview**: Understanding the official standards.
11. **Practice Exercises**: Test your knowledge of accessibility.
