# Screen Reader Testing

The best way to understand if your site is accessible is to use it with a screen reader.

## Popular Screen Readers
-   **NVDA**: Free and open-source for Windows.
-   **JAWS**: Professional standard for Windows (paid).
-   **VoiceOver**: Built into every Mac and iPhone.
-   **Narrator**: Built into Windows.

## Basic Testing Strategy
1.  **Tab through the page**: Can you reach every link, button, and form field?
2.  **Navigate by headings**: Can you jump from section to section easily?
3.  **Listen to images**: Does the alt-text describe what the image is?
4.  **Listen to labels**: Are the form fields announced with their labels?

> [!TIP]
> Try closing your eyes or turning off your monitor for 5 minutes. If you can't navigate your site, neither can many of your users.
