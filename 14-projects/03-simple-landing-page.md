# Project 3: Simple Landing Page

Create a landing page for a fictional product or service.

## Requirements
-   **Hero Section**: A big headline and a "Call to Action" button.
-   **Features**: A section with three columns (or rows) describing the benefits.
-   **Testimonials**: Use `<cite>` and `<blockquote>` for user reviews.
-   **Pricing**: A simple table showing different plans.
-   **Footer**: Links to FAQ, Privacy Policy, and Terms.

## Semantic Checklist
-   Is the "main" content clearly marked?
-   Are the CTA's using `<button>` or `<a>` correctly?
-   Did you use `<section>` for the different parts of the page?
