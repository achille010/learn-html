# Project 1: Personal Portfolio

Create a single-page website that introduces who you are and what you do.

## Requirements
-   **Header**: Include your name and a navigation menu.
-   **About Section**: A paragraph about your background and interests.
-   **Projects Section**: Use a list or a grid of `<article>` tags to show off your work.
-   **Skills**: Use a list to show your technical skills.
-   **Footer**: Include social media links and a copyright notice.

## Semantic Checklist
-   Did you use `<header>`, `<main>`, and `<footer>`?
-   Are your headings ordered correctly (`h1`, then `h2`)?
-   Is your navigation inside a `<nav>` tag?
